Grab headlines with a professional news logo

Viewers and readers have plenty of options when it comes to getting their news. If you want to make sure your TV program or website is the one they turn to for the latest updates, you need a great logo that commands attention and conveys your news focus.

Start by incorporating dynamic colors and shapes into your logo template. These features should be eye-catching while also adding meaning to your design.

For example, red symbolizes urgency, energy, and action, which is great for up-to-the-minute news. If you want to position your brand as an authoritative and trustworthy news source, use blue, which is associated with integrity, honesty, and intelligence.

Shapes can also be used to subtly indicate some of your key brand values. Squares represent stability, balance, and strength, while circles represent community and positivity.

If possible, consider including an icon that indicates your news focus. If you cover international news, you might include an image of the globe. For sports news, you could add a football or baseball to your logo.

Finally, pay close attention to the font. Most news programs and websites use a bold, straightforward typeface to convey a sense of seriousness and professionalism.

Characteristics of news logos

Simplicity

Your news logo will be working with minimal space, so be sure to design a logo that is simple and memorable. You want to design a news logo that conveys your brand’s personality easily, yet effectively.

Colors

Different colors can spark different emotions within a person, and can mean different things in different cultures. Be sure to understand your audience and the emotions you hope to channel through your brand identity.

Relevance

Choose fonts, colors, and symbols that are relevant to the audience you’re trying to reach. Selecting the right combination of these elements in your news logo design will help you to better connect with your market.

Timeless

The best news logos stay memorable and relevant for years. When using our online logo maker, use fonts, basic colors, and symbols that are simple and don’t have unnecessary design elements.
